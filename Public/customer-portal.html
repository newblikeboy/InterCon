How coexistence works

  • The customer keeps the WhatsApp Business app running on their phone.
  • InterCon connects the same number to the Cloud API in coexistence mode — no Cloud API re-registration, so the app stays live.
  • Recent chats and contacts sync so your team can reply from InterCon too.

Requires the WhatsApp Business app on the customer's phone and a Meta app approved for WhatsApp Business app onboarding.
