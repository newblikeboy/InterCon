Ready

Coexistence lets a business keep using the WhatsApp Business app while InterCon adds the Cloud API on the same number — no re-registration, so the app stays live and chat history is preserved.

Before you onboard — eligibility

  • The customer already uses the WhatsApp Business app (version 2.24.17 or newer) on the number.
  • The account is active — brand-new app accounts aren't eligible, and the app must be opened at least once every ~13 days.
  • The number is not already registered on the Cloud API (coexistence keeps it on the app).
  • The number is not a Nigeria or South Africa number — those regions aren't supported by Meta.

How it works

  • Start onboarding — Meta opens a popup for the WhatsApp Business app flow.
  • The customer scans the QR code shown in the popup with their WhatsApp Business app.
  • They can choose to import up to 6 months of chat history and contacts.
  • The number connects to the API in coexistence mode — the app keeps working.

What to expect in coexistence mode

  • Throughput is fixed at 5 messages/second to keep the app and API in sync.
  • Templates can only be sent from the API/InterCon, not from the WhatsApp Business app.
  • Standard marketing messaging limits apply; the Marketing Messages Lite API is not supported.
  • The Official Business Account (green badge) and the Calling API are not supported; apply for Meta Verified instead.
  • Profile-picture updates aren't available after onboarding.
  • Do not uninstall the WhatsApp Business app — it disconnects the account.

Requires a Meta app approved for WhatsApp Business app onboarding (feature whatsapp_business_app_onboarding).
