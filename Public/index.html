Yes. We can support non-technical businesses with onboarding, inbox setup, templates, automation, and training so your sales or support team can use the system comfortably.

Common use cases include lead capture, customer support, order updates, appointment reminders, payment alerts, feedback collection, campaigns, chatbot flows, and CRM or ecommerce integration.
